Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim warranty?
To claim your warranty, contact our support team on WhatsApp and share your Order ID along with a short description of the issue. Our team will verify your order and guide you through the repair process.
What does the 1-year service warranty cover?
It covers repairs for physical damage, hardware issues and software glitches at no service charge. Any replacement spare parts are billed separately. It is a service warranty, not a free replacement or full-cover guarantee.
What is the 1-month checking warranty?
A short one-month warranty to check your device after purchase. It does not cover display, Face ID, software-update issues, or physical and liquid damage.
Is my data safe during a repair?
We take every care with your device, but please back up your data before service. Mobile data can be lost during repair and the shop is not liable for data loss, so we recommend keeping a recent backup.
How long can I leave my device for service?
Please collect your device within 30 days. Verify the handset before leaving the counter, and report any issue within 48 hours of collection so we can assist you quickly.
